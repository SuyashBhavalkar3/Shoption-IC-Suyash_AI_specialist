Founders:

Sharad R. Kale
Founder & CEO:

Sharad R. Kale is an entrepreneur, innovator, and Gold Medalist Software Engineer dedicated to transforming agriculture through technology. As the co-founder of ieyze Corporation Pvt. Ltd., he led the development of over 85 IoT-based irrigation automation products that impacted more than 1.5 million farmers across India and beyond. He later founded Shoption, India's first irrigation-focused B2B e-commerce platform, leveraging AI and patented technologies to empower irrigation retailers and improve access to critical farming solutions. Driven by the belief that every day should be better than the last, Sharad continues to create scalable innovations that strengthen agricultural ecosystems and improve farmers' livelihoods.

journey: Sharad R. Kale grew up as the son of a farmer in a remote village, where his childhood was shaped by the simplicity of nature and the challenges of daily life. Schooling under the shade of trees and studying without electricity exposed him to numerous hardships, yet he never lost sight of his dreams. The first time he encountered a computer was in the 12th grade, a moment that ignited a passion that would ultimately transform his life. Through determination and perseverance, he pursued higher education and graduated as a Gold Medalist in Software Engineering.

Despite receiving lucrative job offers from multinational corporations, Sharad chose a different path—one driven by purpose and a desire to create a lasting impact on the lives of farmers. At the age of 21, while still completing his engineering degree, he co-founded ieyze Corporation Pvt. Ltd. along with a group of college friends. Guided by an unwavering pursuit of excellence and a commitment to meaningful change, the team developed more than 85 IoT-based irrigation automation products. These innovations reached over 1.5 million farmers across 14 states in India, expanded into three additional countries, and supported more than 5,000 agricultural irrigation retailers and distributors across rural Bharat. Under his leadership and vision, ieyze became a trusted name in rural agriculture, fundamentally transforming irrigation practices for farmers.

In June 2020, Sharad exited ieyze by selling his stake to an existing investor, marking the end of one chapter and the beginning of another. Through his experience, he recognized that many challenges faced by farmers originated from the limitations of irrigation retailers, who often struggled to provide essential products and services related to one of farming’s most critical needs—irrigation. This realization led to the creation of Shoption, India’s first irrigation-focused B2B e-commerce platform.

Launched in 2019, Shoption offers a comprehensive digital, multi-brand sourcing experience powered by AI-driven and patented solutions for local consumption-based inventory forecasting, customer mapping, digital unsecured credit, and efficient shop management. The platform enables irrigation shop owners to streamline operations and focus on better serving farmers and their communities.

Guided by the philosophy, “To make today better than yesterday,” Sharad R. Kale continues to inspire others to participate in transforming the lives of the world’s largest community—farmers. His commitment to reshaping the agricultural landscape continues to grow stronger, driven by a mission to bring innovation to the heart of farming and create sustainable impact, one step at a time.
