specifications:Agricultural drone sprayers are autonomous aerial vehicles designed to apply pesticides, herbicides, and fertilizers. By generating a fine mist via rotary or hydraulic nozzles, the powerful downward thrust of the rotors pushes chemicals evenly down to the roots, significantly reducing chemical wastage, water usage, and crop damage.
Drone sprayers vary in tank capacity, flow rate, and payload capability. 
Hexacopter spraying drones are heavy-lift UAVs used for precision agriculture. With six rotors, they offer superior stability and payload capacity. Standard models feature 10L to 30L tanks, autonomous waypoint navigation, and obstacle-avoidance radar.


key features:
6-Rotor Redundancy: Distributes lift across six independent brushless motors. If one motor fails mid-flight, the remaining five maintain control to prevent crashes.Foldable Carbon Fiber Frame: High-strength, lightweight carbon fiber arms fold inward into a classic umbrella design for easy transport in trucks or small vehicles.Integrated Power and Isolation: Wiring is centralized within the main body to separate signal paths from heavy high-voltage power lines, drastically lowering radio interference

Spraying System Features:
Anti-Drip Micro-Atomization Nozzles: Pressure or centrifugal nozzles produce targeted droplet sizes to eliminate runoff and save up to 90% water and 30-40% chemical compared to traditional methods.Variable Flow Pumps: Brushless pumps dynamically adjust the liquid discharge rate to match the ground speed of the drone, ensuring uniform field coverage.Quick-Refill Tanks: Fluid tanks feature sloped, anti-slosh shapes with secure quick-connect ports to minimise downtime during chemical swaps

Avionics & Safety Features:
Terrain-Following Radar: Altimeter radar modules automatically read crop canopy heights, adjusting drone altitude seamlessly over slopes and hilly landscapes.360-Degree Obstacle Radar: Detects poles, wires, trees, and boundaries up to 30 metres away, halting or rerouting the flight path automatically.Autonomous Mission Execution: Supports full waypoint navigation via automated ground station apps, including automatic Return-to-Home (RTH) if batteries drop low or the spray tank empties.
