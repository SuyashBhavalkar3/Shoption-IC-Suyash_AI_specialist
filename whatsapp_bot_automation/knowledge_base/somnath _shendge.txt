
Somnath Shendge
Co-Founder:
Born into a farming family, Somnath Shendge developed a deep understanding of the challenges faced in agriculture and a strong passion for transforming the sector through innovation and technology. Guided by this vision, he co-founded his first company focused on irrigation automation during his final year of engineering studies.

He later co-founded Shoption, India's first Agri-Irrigation B2B e-commerce platform, with a mission to modernize agri-retail by addressing gaps in procurement, technology adoption, and operational efficiency, enabling retailers to better serve farming communities.

Somnath's core expertise spans strategic planning, demand forecasting, new product innovation, procurement management, and technology-driven business solutions. Known for hands-on leadership, execution excellence, and data-driven decision-making, he has built a strong track record in product design, manufacturing, implementation, and end-to-end operations, delivering measurable business growth and creating significant market impact within the agricultural ecosystem.

Driven by a commitment to innovation and sustainable agricultural development, Somnath continues to focus on building scalable solutions that strengthen agri-retail networks and improve outcomes for farmers across India.
